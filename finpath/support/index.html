Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my dashboard showing wrong projections?

Make sure you have added your assets, expenses, and set your goals. The dashboard needs all three to calculate your FIRE date accurately. Tap each tab and verify your data is entered.

How do I backup my data?

Go to Profile → Data Management → Export Data. Save the .json file to Google Drive, email, or any cloud storage. To restore, use Import Data on the same screen.

I forgot my PIN

On the login screen, tap "Forgot PIN?" to delete your profile and start fresh. This permanently erases all your data — make sure you have a backup export first.

What is FIRE?

FIRE stands for Financial Independence, Retire Early. It's a financial strategy where you save and invest aggressively so your investments generate enough passive income to cover all your expenses indefinitely — without needing to work.

What is CSV/PDF export?

FinPath Pro (one-time purchase) lets you export your full year-by-year projection as a CSV or PDF report for deeper analysis in Excel or to share with a financial advisor.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All data is stored only on your device. FinPath has no servers, no cloud sync, and no internet connection. We cannot access your data — structurally impossible.
