Introduction

FinPath ("the App") is a personal finance and retirement planning tool developed by AiHomeCloud. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

FinPath is designed to work entirely offline. Your financial data never leaves your device.

03

How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into FinPath — including your profile, assets, expenses, goals, and calculations — is stored exclusively on your device using local device storage.

  • No data is transmitted to any server
  • No cloud backup is performed by the App
  • No account or login is required
  • Deleting the App removes all data permanently
04

In-App Purchases

FinPath offers an optional in-app purchase to download your financial report. This transaction is processed entirely by the Google Play Store.

AiHomeCloud does not receive, store, or process any payment information. All billing is governed by Google Play's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

05

Third-Party Services

FinPath does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No third party has access to any data entered in the App.

06

Children's Privacy

FinPath is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
