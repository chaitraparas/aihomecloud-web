Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

AiHomeCloud ("we", "our", or "us") is a self-hosted home NAS app. The Android app connects only to a server you set up and control — your own device on your local network, or reached remotely via your own Tailscale network. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

AiHomeCloud does not operate any servers of its own and does not collect personal data for advertising or analytics. The app handles the following data, all of which is sent only to the server you set up — never to AiHomeCloud's developer or any third party:

  • Photos and videos – the app requests photo/video library access solely to let you upload or back up media to your own server, and to browse/stream media already stored there. Files never leave your own device/server pair.
  • Account credentials – the username and password for accounts on your own server, sent over an encrypted (TLS) connection directly to that server.
  • Telegram bot configuration – if you choose to enable Telegram notifications, the bot token you supply is stored only on your own server and used only to send notifications from your own bot.
  • Installer wizard data – when setting up a new server, the app temporarily handles SSH/admin credentials you provide to configure that server. These are used only for the setup connection and are not retained by the app beyond what's needed to manage your session.

2. Third-Party Services

AiHomeCloud includes no advertising SDK, no analytics SDK, and no crash-reporting SDK. All network connections from the app go directly to the server you configured, over a connection secured with TLS and certificate pinning. AiHomeCloud's developer does not operate and has no access to your server or its data.

3. Local & Network Storage Access

The app requests storage/media permissions to browse and upload files from your device, and network permissions (WiFi state, network discovery) to find and connect to your own server on your local network. None of this is used for tracking.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. There are no advertising SDKs, and no analytics or crash reporting services in this app.

5. Data Deletion

All app data — your account, media library, and Telegram configuration — is stored on the server you control, not by AiHomeCloud's developer. You can delete it at any time using the app's own Factory Reset feature, or by removing the data directly from your server. AiHomeCloud's developer holds no copy of this data and cannot delete it on your behalf.

6. Children's Privacy

AiHomeCloud is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
